6 October Club: A New Digital Vision

A comprehensive design proposal to redefine the member experience through a feature-rich, modern mobile application.

Explore the Interactive Prototype

A Unified Club Experience

This proposal consolidates every aspect of the club into a single, intuitive platform. From managing memberships and subscribing to academies, to attending events and engaging with the community, every interaction is designed to be seamless.

Academy Subscriptions

Allow members to easily browse, register, and pay for sports and cultural academies directly within the app.

Events & E-Ticketing

A complete event management system where members can view upcoming events and purchase digital tickets.

Membership Services

Streamline membership renewals, updates, and fee payments with a secure, integrated digital wallet.

QR Code Access Control

Modernize club entry and access to facilities with a dynamic and secure QR code system for members.
